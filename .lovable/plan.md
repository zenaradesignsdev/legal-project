

# William's Legal Services — Multi-Page Website

## Overview
A modern, professional, conversion-focused website for a GTA-based paralegal firm founded by Damian Williams. The site will feature 5 pages with a premium design using deep navy, charcoal, soft gold accents, and an off-white background.

## Design System
- **Colors**: Deep navy primary, charcoal text, soft gold accents, off-white/light beige backgrounds
- **Typography**: Serif headings for authority, clean sans-serif body text
- **Style**: Large spacing, rounded buttons, soft shadows, smooth scroll animations
- **Layout**: Mobile-first responsive design with premium feel

## Pages

### 1. Home Page
- **Hero**: Full-width section with dark overlay, bold headline ("Trusted Legal Representation in the GTA Since 2016"), subheadline, and two CTA buttons (Book Consultation + Call Now) with phone number
- **About Preview**: Brief intro of Damian with his photo and "Meet Our Team" CTA
- **Practice Areas Grid**: 8 modern cards (Provincial Offences, Criminal Law, Landlord & Tenant, Small Claims, Employment Law, Human Rights, Notary Public, Contracts) linking to Services
- **Why Choose Us**: 4 value propositions with icons
- **Consultation CTA**: Free 30-min consultation highlight with pricing info and booking CTA

### 2. Team Page
- Damian Williams featured prominently with his uploaded photo, title, and professional bio
- Space for future team members

### 3. About Page
- Mission statement, firm history since 2016, commitment to access to justice, community involvement, professional standards

### 4. Services Page
- Clean expandable/tabbed sections for all 8 service areas with detailed sub-services
- Notary Public pricing table (tiered pricing from $17 down to $5 per doc)
- Contracts pricing info
- CTA after each section to book consultation

### 5. Contact Page
- Contact info (phone, email, office hours)
- Contact form (Name, Email, Phone, Legal Matter dropdown, Message)
- Google Maps placeholder for GTA area
- Book consultation CTA

## Global Elements
- **Sticky header** with logo, nav links, phone number CTA, and "Book Consultation" button
- **Sticky mobile CTA** button for booking
- **Footer** with quick links, contact info, office hours, and professional legal disclaimer
- **Smooth scroll animations** on section reveals

## Assets
- Damian's uploaded photo will be used on the Team and Home pages
